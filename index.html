Did you know...

Oscillating sprinkler loses almost 50% of water to the atmosphere before it reaches your plants and lawn. It happens because it shoots water drops in the air so high that by the time they reach your plants, half of it evaporates.

Many times during hand watering we deliver water too fast so the soil can’t absorb it in right proportions and it becomes oversaturated. It is an inefficient way to water and in many cases watested water running onto driveways and streets.

The automatic irrigation system design provides efficient watering lawns and gardens and enhances plant health with no need of dragging hoses. It is designed for application of right amount of water in the best possible time. Carefully planned and designed system allows for 80% of water to the plants instead of atmosphere.

What is irrigation?

Irrigation system is an artificial application of water and it is design to keep all parts of your garden evenly moisture through the growing season. The water level is crucial for ap- propriate health of your plants because you don’t want soil to become too wet or too dry.

Water Conservation

For many people using the automatic irrigation system may seem wasteful; however, it actually uses less water than traditional hand watering. Therefore, water conservation should be your principle in watering system design in your garden.
